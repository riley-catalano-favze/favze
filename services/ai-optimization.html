Understanding AI Optimization

What Is AI Optimization, and Why Does Your Business Need It Right Now?

Here's the plain-English version. No technical jargon, no buzzwords — just a clear explanation of what's changing and what it means for businesses in Northern Indiana and Michiana.

The shift that's already happening

A few years ago, finding a local business was simple: you searched Google, clicked a link, and called. That process is changing. Today, a growing number of people open ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, or Siri and simply ask: "Who's the best plumber in Goshen?" or "What's a good dentist near South Bend?"

These AI tools don't show you a list of links to click through. They give you a direct answer — a specific business name, a short description, and sometimes even a phone number or website. They decide who to recommend, and they don't explain their reasoning to the person asking.

If your business isn't set up to be recommended, you won't be. The customer moves on, calls the competitor the AI mentioned, and never knows you exist.

How AI decides who to recommend

AI tools pull from everything they can find about your business across the internet. They read your website content, your Google Business Profile, your reviews, directory listings, local news mentions, and the way other authoritative sites reference you. Then they make a judgment call about which business is the most credible, relevant, and trustworthy answer to the question being asked.

AI Optimization is the process of shaping all of those signals so the AI consistently lands on your name. It's not about tricking the system. It's about making sure the truth about your business — how good you are, who you serve, where you're located — is presented in a way that AI can find, read, and trust.

Favze combines two proven strategies to do this: Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). Both are explained in plain terms below.

Strategy One

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

AEO focuses on how your content appears in search results and AI answer boxes — making your business the top result for the queries that matter to you.

Featured snippets: Structuring your content to appear as Google's top direct answer above all other results.
People Also Ask (PAA): Answering the exact questions your customers are searching for, in the format Google's algorithm rewards.
Schema & structured data: Adding machine-readable code that helps AI understand who you are, what you do, and where you do it.
Voice search readiness: Formatting content so smart devices — Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant — can read it cleanly as a spoken answer.
Local question targeting: Capturing "near me" and city-specific queries that reflect real buyer intent in Goshen, Elkhart, South Bend, and across the Michiana region.
Strategy Two

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

GEO focuses on how AI tools use and cite your content when generating responses — ensuring your business is trusted and recommended by the AI itself.

AI-friendly formatting: Clear headings, logical structure, and organized content that AI models can parse and confidently cite.
Semantic & contextual optimization: Ensuring AI understands what your business does, who it serves, and why it's the best answer for local queries.
Authority building: Proper citations, verified business information, and high-quality references that increase AI trust in your content.
Entity recognition: Making sure AI identifies your business name, location, service categories, and key differentiators as distinct, trusted entities.
Up-to-date content maintenance: Ensuring AI always references accurate, current information about your business — not outdated details that erode trust.

Why both strategies work better together: AEO makes your content discoverable — it puts you in front of AI tools. GEO makes your content authoritative — it makes AI tools choose you as the answer. Without both, you're either invisible to AI or visible but not trusted enough to be recommended. Schema Markup & Structured Data

Schema markup is code added to your website that tells AI and search engines precisely what your business is, where it operates, what it offers, and why it's trustworthy. Without it, AI tools have to guess — and they often guess wrong or not at all. With it, your business data is machine-readable and AI-eligible.

LocalBusiness schema with your full service area, categories, hours, contact info, and geographic coverage
Service schema for every individual service you offer, structured for both AI and Google Rich Results
FAQPage schema that makes your Q&A content directly readable by AI recommendation engines
Review and rating schema so AI platforms can reference your reputation as a verified trust signal
Author and organization schema to establish entity authority for your brand
