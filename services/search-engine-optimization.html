The Foundation of Every SEO Strategy

SEO Has Three Layers. Favze Works All of Them.

Most agencies focus on one piece and ignore the rest. Favze builds Northern Indiana SEO strategies that address every layer of the ecosystem, because rankings are earned when all three work together, not just one.

01
Layer 01

On-Page SEO

On-page SEO is everything that happens on your actual website: the content, the structure, the keywords, and the signals that tell Google exactly what each page is about and who it should be shown to. Get this wrong and nothing else matters.

  • Title tags and meta descriptions optimized for clicks and keywords
  • Header structure (H1 to H4) correctly built on every page
  • Keyword integration that reads naturally and ranks reliably
  • Image alt text and URL structure cleaned up and optimized
  • Internal linking structured to flow authority to your most important pages
  • Content formatted for readability, engagement, and conversion

On-page is the layer your Northern Indiana competitors are most likely to have done poorly. It's also the layer Favze fixes first, because it makes everything else more effective.

02
Layer 02

Technical SEO

Technical SEO is the infrastructure your entire strategy runs on. If Google cannot efficiently crawl, index, and trust your website, the best content and backlinks in the world will not move the needle. Favze audits and resolves every technical issue holding your site back.

  • Core Web Vitals: LCP, CLS, and INP brought to Google's passing thresholds
  • Mobile-first optimization on every page and every device
  • Crawl efficiency and index control so Google finds what matters
  • Broken links, redirects, and 404 errors resolved
  • Schema markup and structured data so Google understands your content
  • XML sitemaps, robots.txt, and canonicalization properly configured

A technically broken site cannot rank no matter how strong the content or backlinks are. Favze resolves these invisible barriers before putting effort anywhere else.

03
Layer 03

Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO is how Google measures your authority and trustworthiness based on signals that exist outside your website. Backlinks from credible sources, brand mentions, and citation consistency all tell Google your Northern Indiana business deserves to rank.

  • High-quality backlink acquisition from relevant, authoritative sources
  • Natural anchor text distribution that avoids over-optimization penalties
  • Brand authority and citation building across Northern Indiana directories
  • Toxic backlink identification and disavow strategy for damaged profiles
  • Content-driven link earning through assets worth referencing organically
  • No spam. No shortcuts. No link schemes that create future penalties.

One link from a respected regional publication is worth more than a hundred low-quality directory links. Favze focuses on quality over quantity, building authority your Michiana business can grow on for years.

All three layers working together is what earns page one. Most Northern Indiana businesses are missing at least one. Some are missing all three. Favze's free consultation identifies exactly where your site stands across each layer so you know what needs to happen before a single dollar is spent.
