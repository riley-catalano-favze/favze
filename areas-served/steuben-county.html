Who We Are

The Agency Built for Steuben County's Distinct Markets.

Steuben County holds a unique position in Indiana's northeast corner: it borders both Ohio and Michigan, contains more lakes than any other county in the state, and operates a dual economy that most Northern Indiana counties don't — a year-round residential and university-driven commercial base in Angola alongside a seasonal lake visitor economy that stretches from the Chain O' Lakes through Lake James and beyond. Most Steuben County businesses serve both audiences and are invisible to both in digital search.

Indiana's Lake Country and the Seasonal Visitor Pipeline
With over 100 lakes — including Lake James, Snow Lake, and the Chain O' Lakes State Park — Steuben County draws cottage owners, campers, anglers, and seasonal vacationers from Fort Wayne, Indianapolis, and across the Midwest every spring and summer. These visitors and second-home owners research everything before they arrive: boat rentals, restaurants, grocery and bait shops, lawn care, home services, and entertainment. Steuben County businesses that don't appear in that pre-trip research process hand the sale to whichever competitor does. The gap between visitor search volume and local business digital visibility in Steuben County is one of the clearest missed opportunities in all of Northern Indiana.

Angola — the county seat — runs on a different engine. Trine University anchors the city with thousands of students, faculty, and staff whose commercial needs fuel year-round demand for food, services, retail, and off-campus housing. The US-20 commercial corridor serves county residents, travelers, and the growing professional services base that supports the region. Fremont in the north, Hamilton near the DeKalb County line, and Orland in the northwest round out a county where rural agricultural communities sit minutes from popular lake destinations.

Favze is based in Middlebury in Elkhart County, operating across the same Northern Indiana corridor that includes Steuben County. We understand how this regional market functions — from the seasonal rhythms of the lake economy to the year-round commercial dynamics of a university town. That regional fluency is built into every strategy we build for Steuben County clients.
