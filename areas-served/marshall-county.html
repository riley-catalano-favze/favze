What's the difference between SEO, Local SEO, AEO, and GEO?

SEO builds your organic authority in Google's general search results over time. Local SEO specifically targets Google Maps and the Local 3-Pack — the map results that appear when someone searches "restaurant near me" or "contractor in Plymouth Indiana." For any Marshall County business with a physical location or defined service area, Local SEO is often the single highest-impact investment available.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures your content to be pulled as a direct answer by Google AI Overviews, Siri, and Alexa. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) builds the authority signals that cause AI tools like ChatGPT to mention and recommend your business when someone asks about Marshall County services. All four layers reinforce and compound each other.
