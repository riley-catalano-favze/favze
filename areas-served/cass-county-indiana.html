Who We Are

The Agency Built for Cass County’s Regional Economy.

Logansport is the largest city along the US 24 corridor between Fort Wayne and Lafayette, and it functions as a genuine regional hub. Cass County Memorial Health, the county courthouse, and a manufacturing base that includes major employers like Delphi Technologies draw workers and customers from well outside the county line. The Eel River meets the Wabash right at Logansport, and the city’s historic carousel, one of only a few antique carousels still in operation in Indiana, anchors Riverside Park as a genuine community landmark. Downtown Logansport has the bones of a real commercial district and a growing number of independently-owned businesses working to reclaim the corridor. Galveston and Walton serve their own rural communities on the northern end of the county. Royal Center anchors the west side with the Tippecanoe River running nearby. Cass County is not a saturated digital marketing market. Most categories across the county are wide open. The first businesses to build genuine presence will hold those positions for years.
