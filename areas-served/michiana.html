The Michiana Region

The Agency Built for Michiana's Unique Markets.

Michiana is not a single market — it's a collection of deeply distinct economies sharing a geography. St. Joseph County runs on Notre Dame's global academic gravity and South Bend's urban commercial core. Elkhart County is the world's RV manufacturing capital, with a manufacturing workforce and a distribution economy that operates on its own schedule. LaGrange County hosts Indiana's largest Amish community, driving a visitor economy in Shipshewana that draws tourism from across the Midwest. Marshall County's Plymouth and the agricultural townships around it serve a production farming base. Kosciusko County's Warsaw is globally significant in orthopedic device manufacturing. LaPorte County opens onto Lake Michigan, anchoring a shoreline tourism economy in Michigan City with a very different customer profile than the inland counties.

Michiana Crosses a State Line
Berrien County and Cass County in southwest Michigan are part of Michiana's natural market — economically, culturally, and geographically. Niles, Benton Harbor, Cassopolis, and Dowagiac are part of the same regional economy as South Bend and Elkhart. Favze serves both Michigan counties with the same full-service approach as every Indiana county in the region. If your business is in Michigan but operates in the Michiana orbit, you're covered.

The cross-state dimension of Michiana also creates marketing complexity that most regional agencies miss entirely. A Niles, Michigan business competes with South Bend businesses for the same customers. A Berrien Springs service provider serves both Michigan and Indiana households. A Cassopolis retailer draws from a radius that crosses the state line regularly. Understanding which audience is actually searching for your business — and where — requires genuine regional fluency, not a county-by-county template.

Favze is based in Middlebury in Elkhart County — at the geographic and economic center of the Michiana region. We live and operate in this market. Every strategy we build for Michiana clients is grounded in how this specific region actually works.
