What We Actually Believe

This is what

These are not corporate values on a wall. These are the specific beliefs that determine what Favze sells, how we report, when we push back, and why the agency exists at all.

01
Buyers, not Followers.

Follower count is the most misleading metric in marketing. A business with 4,000 followers and empty tables on Tuesday has a problem, not a success story. Favze builds for all three buyer intent tiers: high intent, medium intent, and unaware. Because every one of them can become a customer.

02
Transparent pricing.
Always.

Every cost is visible before you sign anything. No setup fees buried in the contract. No overages billed after the fact. No line items that require an explanation you didn't ask for. You budget for marketing the same way you budget for anything else with confidence in the number.

03
Reports you can read.

Dense dashboards built to look impressive rather than inform are not accountability they're a power imbalance. Favze reports answer the questions business owners actually have: Is this working? Where's the money going? What changed and why? Plain language. Real numbers.

04
The consultation
isn't a sales call.

It's a conversation about your business. What you're trying to accomplish. What's been tried. What your budget looks like. The goal is to figure out whether Favze can actually help you and be honest if the answer is no. Good marketing starts with listening, not pitching.

05
One strategy.
Not six vendors.

Most businesses that say marketing isn't working are running marketing that isn't connected. Their SEO agency doesn't talk to their social person. Their ads don't align with their email. One team. One strategy. No finger-pointing when something isn't working.

06
Hard lines we
won't cross.

No lock-in before clarity. No jargon to dodge accountability. No going quiet when results dip. You'll hear from Favze first with an explanation and a plan. No selling services you don't need. No vanity metrics in reports. These are the baseline, not aspirations.

07 The Community Belief
Even if you're not a client, you deserve to succeed online.

Favze shares educational content across all platforms for everyone, not just clients. Local businesses win when the whole community learns to grow. That is not a marketing tactic. That is the belief behind Favze Insights.

Modern Visibility Explained

Getting Found Has Changed.
Most Agencies Haven't.

Ranking on Google still matters. But AI now answers a growing share of searches directly citing businesses by name. Favze builds visibility across all of it.

Channel 01

Search Engine
Optimization.

Google doesn't reward quality alone. It rewards structure, authority, and intent alignment. Ranking requires technical precision that most in-house teams don't have time to master: site architecture, schema markup, content hierarchy, local citation authority, and competitive keyword strategy all working together.

Favze handles technical SEO, on-page optimization, local SEO, and content strategy as integrated work, not separate services that don't talk to each other. Your website should be a lead-generating machine, not just a digital brochure.

72% of consumers search online before visiting a business. If you're not on page one for your key terms in your market, you're not in the race.

Channel 02

Answer Engine
Optimization.

AI doesn't list options it selects answers. If your content isn't structured for retrieval, schema, and citation validation, you simply don't exist in AI-driven search. Google AI Overviews already appear above organic results for millions of searches every day.

AEO structures your content so answer engines like Google AI Overviews, Siri, and Alexa cite your business when answering questions in your category. Favze connects this content across blogs, social media, and digital channels for maximum visibility.

Local searches like "best contractor near me" are increasingly answered by AI before any human clicks a link. AEO puts you in those answers.

Channel 03

Generative Engine
Optimization.

When someone asks ChatGPT "who's the best plumber in Elkhart County" the system recommends businesses by name. Not a list of links. It chooses. Generative engines prioritize recognized entities with digital authority. If your brand lacks semantic reinforcement across the web, AI cannot confidently recommend you.

GEO builds your brand into the AI's understanding of your category and geography so that when customers ask generative AI for recommendations in your market, your business is the one it names. Most agencies aren't doing this yet.

ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity are recommending local businesses by name. Most Northern Indiana businesses aren't in those recommendations yet. That's a solvable problem.

Channel 04

AI
Optimization.

AI Optimization is the technical foundation underneath AEO and GEO. It ensures your business information is accurate, structured, and consistent across every AI-powered platform so each system can find you, understand what you do, and confidently place you in its answers.

This includes business entity optimization, knowledge graph signals, cross-platform NAP consistency, structured data markup, and authoritative citation building. AI without integration creates speed, not leverage. Real results require workflow alignment and visibility integration.

Most agencies are still thinking about search the way it worked five years ago. AI Optimization is how Favze clients stay ahead of competitors who haven't realized the game has changed.
